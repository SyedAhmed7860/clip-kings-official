Booking Policies & FAQ

What happens if I'm late? +
We grant a strict 10-minute grace period. If you arrive later than 10 minutes past your scheduled time, your appointment may be cancelled or modified to a shorter service to respect the next client's time and maintain our high standards.
Do you accept walk-ins? +
While we accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking in advance. Our master barbers are typically fully booked for the day, and reserving a spot guarantees your time.
What is your Cancellation Policy? +
Please cancel or reschedule at least 12 hours in advance via the Booksy app or by calling the shop. No-shows or late cancellations disrupt the schedule and may be subject to a fee applied on your next visit.
Do I need to wash my hair before arriving? +
It is always appreciated if you arrive with clean hair free of heavy products (like thick pomades or gels). However, if you select one of our VIP Packages, a premium hair wash is already included in the service.
