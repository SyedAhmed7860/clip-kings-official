Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At Clip Kings Barber Shop, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and safeguarding of your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, purchase a gift card, or interact with our digital presence, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address.
  • Appointment Data: Booking history, service preferences, barber preferences.
  • Digital Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction metrics on our website (collected via cookies).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is utilized strictly to provide a premium luxury experience. This includes:

  • Processing your Booksy reservations and payments securely.
  • Sending appointment confirmations and reminders.
  • Personalizing your in-shop grooming experience based on past preferences.
  • Communicating exclusive Happy Hour deals or VIP Membership offers (only if opted-in).

3. Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. All booking and payment data is processed through our secure, industry-standard third-party partner, Booksy, ensuring enterprise-grade encryption and compliance.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections, or request deletion of your data from our systems. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us directly at privacy@clipkings.com.
